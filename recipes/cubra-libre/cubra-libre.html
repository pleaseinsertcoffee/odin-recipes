Cubra Libre

Rum and Coke improvement

I'm not much of a drinker, but if I was.....

Ingredients

  • 2 fluid ounces of rum
  • 1/2 fluid ounce of lime juice
  • 3/4 cup of ice
  • 2 fluid ounces of cola flavoured soda
  1. Combine rum and lime juice in a highball glass. Top with ice and fill with cola.
